Refund Policy

At Klifora, we strive to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a money-back guarantee.

1. Eligibility:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • The item must be in its original condition and accompanied by the original receipt.
  • Custom-made or engraved items are not eligible for a refund.

2. Process:

  • Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.
  • Ship the item back to us using the provided return shipping label.
  • Once we receive the item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 7-10 business days.

3. Refund Method:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment.

4. Shipping Costs:

  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error.
